What is "Teach Me How to Scream"?

Teach Me How to Scream is an online vocal coach application powered by AI and artists.

Do I need prior vocal training to use this site?

Some knowledge may be necessary, however the coaches on this site are professionals trained with plenty of knowledge to further guide you.

How does AI help in learning to scream/growl?

Our scoring system allows you to compare your vocals to other coaches with a rating and feedback. The data collected from vocalists also goes into a machine learning process to better our algorithms as well as provide data for scientific studies on heavy vocals.

What equipment do I need to get started?

Though you can use your computer's built-in microphone, you're better off using something more professional or with decent quality as the quality of your mic will affect the algorithm.

Is the platform compatible with mobile devices?

Not at the moment but hopefully in the future!!!

How do I troubleshoot microphone issues?

Ensure your microphone is configured in your browser settings. Follow these steps for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Microphone. Select your USB microphone and ensure permissions are enabled.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Navigate to Settings > Privacy & Security > Permissions > Microphone. Select your USB microphone and allow access.
  • Microsoft Edge: Open Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions > Microphone. Choose your USB microphone and grant access.
  • Safari (macOS): Go to Preferences > Websites > Microphone. Ensure the site is set to Allow, and verify your microphone in System Preferences > Sound.

If the issue persists, ensure your USB microphone is properly connected, recognized by your computer, and test it on another application to verify functionality.

What are the different vocal styles I can learn here?

Fry's, Yells, False Chord, Gutturals, Pig Squeals... You name it. As the community continues to develop there will be plenty heavy vocal techniques to choose from.

How do I know if my technique is correct?

Don't strain yourself, if you feel comfortable the better your technique will be. Keep practicing against the algorithm and boost your scores. If you need visual tutorials, visit the artist's page and see if they have tutorials that fit your needs.

Can I save or download my recordings?

Yes! You can download samples from vocal coaches as well as your own. For vocal coaches, look for the orange download button in the upper left-hand corner of the main page. For your recordings, the button is located under the User Info section in the lower right-hand corner of the screen.

How do I edit my profile?

Basic users don't have profiles yet but do have the ability to upload a profile picture. For Artist or Coach accounts, visit your page and the entire page is interactive for your profile needs.

Can I update my profile picture?

Yes! Basic users get only one photo at the moment as further refinements are still being implemented on the site. However, Artist or Coach accounts can change your profile picture simply by clicking on it.

Is my data safe with this site?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. We use third-party providers like Recurly for subscriptions and PayPal for donations. Vocal data is collected for processing but never sold to third-parties.

Is this platform free to use?

For now, but in the future, it will cost.

Are there premium features available?

Vocal Coach accounts are as premium as you can get right now. However, the site is still under development and further refinements may end up creating other features and possibly premium accounts.

What payment methods are accepted?

How do I connect with other users?

You can visit vocal coaches' pages by clicking their profile picture, and there you should see a questions tab where you can ask other artists questions about anything regarding your vocal needs or even just a shout out.

Is there a community forum?

Not yet, but in the future, there should be.
